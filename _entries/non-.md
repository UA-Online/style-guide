---
title: non-
section: Word Choice
---
Usually not hyphenated when used before a word starting with a consonant, e.g., _nontraditional, nonrefundable, noncompliance, nondegree_, _noncredit_.[^1] Use a hyphen before proper nouns or in awkward combinations, such as _non-nuclear_.[^2] Consult the Merriam-Webster dictionary for more guidance.
